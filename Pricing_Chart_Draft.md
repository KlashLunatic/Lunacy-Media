Based on market research for high-end boutique creative agencies in Toronto, this framework balances competitive rates with the "mythic" and "strategic" value of Lunacy Media.

| Tier | Service Level | Price Range (CAD) | Ideal For | Key Deliverables |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **The Signal** | **Foundational Identity** | **$10,000 – $15,000** | Early-stage founders & independent artists. | Core Visual Identity, Logo System, Typography, Basic Brand Guidelines. |
| **The Orbit** | **Strategic Worldbuilding** | **$20,000 – $35,000** | Established brands & creative collectives. | Brand Mythology, Full Visual System, Pitch Deck/Investor Materials, Website Design. |
| **The Universe** | **Full-Scale Ecosystem** | **$50,000 – $100,000+** | Organizations & IP Developers. | Original IP Development, Multi-channel Campaign, Custom Web Platform, Content Production (Video/Audio). |
| **Consulting** | **Strategic Direction** | **$5,000 – $10,000 / mo** | Long-term partners. | Ongoing Creative Direction, Brand Workshops, Narrative Strategy. |

### **Notes on Pricing Logic:**
*   **Mythic Value:** We don't just "design"; we "architect." The pricing reflects the strategic depth and narrative worldbuilding that goes into every project.
*   **Toronto Market:** These rates are positioned at the mid-to-high end of boutique agencies in Toronto, signaling quality and exclusivity.
*   **Scalability:** Each tier is designed to be a "building block" for the next, allowing clients to grow within the Lunacy orbit.
